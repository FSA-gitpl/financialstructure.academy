An independent educational unit built on market structure.

Financial Structure Academy was created in response to the lack of coherent, technical, and structural market education.

FSA Products

Knowledge modules based on market structure.

FSA products — books and courses — are designed as coherent knowledge modules. Each module develops a specific element of the methodology: structure, liquidity, participants, process, or decision.

Their content is created by an international team of traders with 5–15 years of experience across different markets. This is practical knowledge tested in real conditions.

The goal of FSA materials is to increase user competence and reduce the number of errors made at the beginning of the learning path.
