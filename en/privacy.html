Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how Financial Structure Academy ("FSA", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website or use our digital products. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data.

Financial Structure Academy does not create or maintain user accounts on our platform. We collect only the minimum amount of data necessary to process purchases and provide support.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly:

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you purchase a product or contact us. This includes:

  • Name and Email Address (required for product delivery and support).
  • Payment information (processed securely by third-party gateways; we do not store credit card details).
  • Communications sent to our support email.

Automatically Collected Information:

When you visit our website, our servers automatically record standard log information. This includes:

  • IP Address and general location (country/city level).
  • Browser type, operating system, and referring URLs.
  • Date and time of access.

This data is used solely for security purposes (e.g., preventing fraud) and technical optimization. It is not used for marketing profiling.

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information for the following specific purposes:

  • To process and deliver digital products immediately after purchase.
  • To communicate with you regarding your order or support inquiries.
  • To maintain the security and integrity of our website.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.

3. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses basic cookies essential for functionality (e.g., maintaining session states, theme preferences). We do not use intrusive tracking cookies or third-party analytics scripts that build behavioral profiles of users.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain features, such as the dark/light mode toggle.

4. Third-Party Service Providers

We engage trusted third-party services to perform specific functions on our behalf. These services have access to your personal information only as necessary to perform their tasks and are obligated not to disclose it to others.

  • Payment Processors (e.g., Stripe): Handle financial transactions. They adhere to PCI-DSS standards. We do not store your financial data.
  • Email Service Providers: Used to deliver purchase confirmations and digital files securely.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, including legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

If you make a purchase, your email and transaction details are retained for accounting and customer support purposes. You may request the deletion of this data by contacting us, subject to legal retention obligations.

6. Security of Your Data

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our website is secured with SSL encryption (HTTPS).

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., GDPR in the EU, CCPA in California), you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access your data.
  • The right to correct inaccurate data.
  • The right to request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • The right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the email provided below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services and digital products are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
